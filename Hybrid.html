Why hybrid marijuana strains are so popular?

All hybrid strains are created artificially, which allows enhancing new products with better properties, taking them to new heights that were unreachable for originals. As a general rule, new varieties possess hereditary traits received from their “ancestors.” This opens practically limitless opportunities for experimenting with strains and creating new hybrid weed for sale on the mass market. As you may know, Indica is the best choice for relaxing, soothing your pain sensations and slowing down brain activity, while Sativa gives you a boost of activity while putting your brain into the active state. These effects are polar, but with a hybrid strain of weed, you can balance them out as you want. For example, if you want to be just a little bit more active during your day, pure Sativa would be too much – you simply might get carried away. In this case, you should better go with a hybrid that is mostly Sativa but also contains some Indica. This way, the effect won’t be too extreme. The best part about hybrid strains is the ability to experiment. You can always choose the top-notch hybrid weed strains for any particular situation by simply correcting the percentage of Indica or Sativa in the product.
